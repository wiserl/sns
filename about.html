About

Safe and Sound Home Inspection was founded by industry powerhouses with an attitude of a “We care” instilled in the company's core business philosophy. We strive to guarantee a 5 star experience for every client, and are maticulouse about providing a streamlined customer experience. With a partnership and backing of Home Base Appraisal Management, we are also able to provide a corporate infrastructure of robust features and service, all with a local feel and touch.

Scott Gaston(Chief Home Inspector): J.Scott Gaston has been in the construction trades for over 20 years. He started in Structural Steel where he, as a journeymen Ironworker, lead teams to finish large scale operations on time and on schedule. Scott continued in his trade and opened his own General contracting business. Combining his structural steel and contracting license his work has been featured in Better Homes and Gardens, Parade of Homes and many other publications. Scott has been inspecting for over a year and with his many years of construction trades backgrounds gives him a unique perspective ensuring your home is properly inspected and follows regulations. As a proud father of 3 very active boys, Scott spends his time at the Baseball diamond where his boys are approached by college and pro scouts. He enjoys being outside using the time to hunt, fish and camp when he can. He also is an amazing cook with his smoked meats and Dutch oven recipes that are requested and purchased by numerous individuals.

Michael Jorgensen(CEO): Michael Jorgensen is a founder CEO of Safe & Sound Inspection & Home Base Appraisal Management, a nation AMC that provides appraisals and valuation products. His companies have been recognized twice by Mountain West Capital Network as one of Utah's fastest growing companies, and four time INC 5000 fastest growing companies in America. Michael a young innovative mind, who was recognized by utah business magazine as one of Utah's 20 in their 20's, and has been on the front lines of new real estate products, & custom software in the industry for over 10 years. He has worked hard to establish a "we care" attitude that not only rings true for his team, but all people he comes in contact with. Michael is married with two children on the way.

We here at Safe and Sound Home Inspections are pleased to have Scott & Michael lead our team.

*Safe and Sound currently provides home inspection services the following counties: Salt Lake, Utah, Davis, Weber, Summit, and Heber*
