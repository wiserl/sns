About

J.Scott Gaston has been in the construction trades for over 20 years. He started in Structural Steel where he, as a journeymen Ironworker, lead teams to finish large scale operations on time and on schedule. Scott continued in his trade and opened his own General contracting business. Combining his structural steel and contracting license his work has been featured in Better Homes and Gardens, Parade of Homes and many other publications. Scott has been inspecting for over a year and with his many years of construction trades backgrounds gives him a unique perspective ensuring your home is properly inspected and follows regulations. As a proud father of 3 very active boys, Scott spends his time at the baseball diamond where his boys are approached by college and pro scouts. He enjoys being outside using the time to hunt, fish and camp when he can. He also is an amazing cook with his smoked meats and Dutch oven recipes that are requested and purchased by numerous individuals.
